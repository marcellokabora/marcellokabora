The Brief

Prep time: ~60 minutes | Presentation: 25-30 minutes

Context

Dash0 is an observability platform for developers. The market is crowded and most websites in this space look and sound the same. We're rebuilding dash0.com from the ground up - not just as a marketing site, but as a full marketing engine and information hub that serves every route to Dash0: self-serve sign-ups, product-led growth, direct sales, and everything in between.

We want the new site to feel unmistakably Dash0 - bold, distinctive, and built to convert the right audience.

Your Task

Find three to five websites that have caught your attention. Don't limit yourself to dev tools or observability. What are they doing that works? What's the gap you think Dash0 could own? Put together a small moodboard - screenshots, links, references, whatever you naturally reach for. We're not looking for a finished direction, just a sense of where you'd start and why.

What would you ship first? Look at the current dash0.com. What are two or three specific things you'd change or ship in your first few weeks, before the full rebuild gets going? These should be concrete enough that you could hand them off to someone tomorrow. Be ready to speak to why: what metric moves, what signal does it set?

How would you think about turning dash0.com into a site that actively generates high quality leads? What would you add, instrument, or change to make it work harder as a marketing engine, not just a front door?

Walk us through how you'd actually run this project. How do you kick it off? What do you need to know early, and what can wait? How do you keep momentum without getting stuck waiting for decisions or input? And how do you know when something is good enough to ship?

How to share your ideas

No specific format required, use whatever helps you think and communicate clearly. A Figma board, slides, a doc, or even just pulling up browser tabs works fine.
